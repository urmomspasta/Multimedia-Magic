1. What Information Do We Collect?

At Multimedia Magic, we collect two main types of information. First, we gather information you provide, including personal details like your name, email, postal address, phone number, and account credentials (username and password). If you make purchases, we collect payment information such as credit card numbers, but please note that we use trusted third-party payment processors to handle this data, and we do not store your payment information. Second, we automatically collect device and usage information when you visit our website, including your IP address, browser type, and data related to how you interact with our services. We also utilize cookies and tracking technologies to enhance your experience and understand your use of our website.

2. How Do We Use Your Information?

We process your information for several purposes, such as facilitating account creation, sending marketing and promotional communications (you can opt out at any time), managing orders, posting testimonials (with your consent), delivering targeted advertising, administering competitions, requesting feedback, enforcing terms, responding to legal requests, and providing user support. We also use your data for various business purposes, including data analysis, identifying usage trends, and evaluating and improving our services.

3. Who Will Your Information Be Shared With?

We share your information only under specific circumstances, including with your consent, to fulfill our legitimate business interests, for contractual obligations, and to meet legal requirements. Third-party service providers like payment processors, vendors, and consultants may access your data when providing services on our behalf. In case of mergers or business transfers, we may share or transfer your data. Our affiliates and business partners may also receive your information for offering products and services.

4. Do We Use Cookies and Other Tracking Technologies?

Yes, we use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your website experience. These technologies help us gather data about how you use our website.

5. How Long Do We Keep Your Information?

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary for the purposes mentioned in this policy or as required by law. Your data will be deleted or anonymized when we no longer have a legitimate business need to process it.

6. How Do We Keep Your Information Safe?

Multimedia Magic employs technical and organizational security measures to protect your data. However, remember that the internet isn't entirely secure, and you access our services at your own risk.

7. Do We Collect Information from Minors?

No, we do not knowingly collect data from or market to children under 18 years of age. If you are under 18, you should have a parent or guardian's consent to use our services.

8. What Are Your Privacy Rights?

If you are in the European Economic Area or under similar data protection laws, you may have certain privacy rights. These rights may include accessing, modifying, or deleting your personal information, withdrawing your consent, and lodging complaints with your local data protection authority.

9. Data Breach

We take data breaches seriously and will notify you when we believe your information is at risk or has been breached, as per legal requirements.

10. Controls for Do-Not-Track Features

Currently, we do not respond to Do-Not-Track signals or similar mechanisms for online tracking.

11. Do We Make Updates to This Policy?

Yes, we may update this policy to remain compliant with relevant laws. We will inform you of significant changes through notice or direct communication.
